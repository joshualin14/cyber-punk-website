6 Street

The 6th Street Gang in Cyberpunk 2077 began as a vigilante group formed by ex-military veterans after the devastation of the 4th Corporate War. Disillusioned with the chaos and abandonment left by the megacorps, they originally swore to protect their neighborhoods and provide the security the authorities failed to deliver. Over time, however, their ideals corroded. What started as a community defense f orce gradually twisted into a ruthless gang, exploiting the very people they once swore to protect. Today, 6th Street still claims the mantle of guardianship, but their methods—extortion, intimidation, and violence—reveal how far they’ve strayed from their noble roots.
